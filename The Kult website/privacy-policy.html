Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

1. Introduction

The Kult Music Group ("The Kult," "we," "us," or "our") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting any personal information you share with us. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect through our website, why we collect it, how we use it, and the choices you have regarding your data.

2. Information We Collect

When you submit a campaign request through our contact form, we collect the following information that you voluntarily provide:

  • Your full name
  • Your artist or project name
  • Your email address
  • Your Spotify link (if provided)
  • Your musical genre (if provided)
  • Your campaign budget range
  • Your planned release date (if provided)
  • Any message or additional details you choose to include

We do not knowingly collect sensitive personal information, payment card details, or government identification numbers through this form.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information you submit solely to:

  • Respond to your campaign inquiry and provide a tailored proposal
  • Communicate with you about your potential or active campaign
  • Maintain internal records of client inquiries

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes.

4. How Your Information Is Processed

Submissions made through our contact form are transmitted using EmailJS, a third-party email delivery service, directly to our business inbox. EmailJS processes this data solely to deliver the email and does not use it for any other purpose. We recommend reviewing EmailJS's own privacy practices at emailjs.com/legal/privacy-policy for further detail on their handling of transmitted data.

5. Data Retention

We retain submitted inquiry information for as long as reasonably necessary to respond to your request and maintain a record of client communications, or until you request deletion as described below.

6. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights under applicable data protection laws (including the EU/UK GDPR, South Africa's POPIA, or the California CCPA) to:

  • Request access to the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your personal information
  • Object to or restrict our processing of your information

To exercise any of these rights, contact us using the details below. We will respond to verified requests within a reasonable timeframe.

7. Cookies and Analytics

Our website does not currently use cookies or third-party analytics tracking. If this changes in the future, this policy will be updated to reflect that.

8. Children's Privacy

Our services are intended for artists, managers, and businesses, and are not directed at individuals under the age of 16. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for legal reasons. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page will reflect the most recent revision.

10. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or wish to exercise your data rights, please contact us at theonlykult@gmail.com.
