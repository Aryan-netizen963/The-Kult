Common Questions

We brief vetted TikTok creators to make organic-feeling content using your track's sound. As videos gain traction, viewers search for the song on Spotify — driving streams, saves, and follows that compound over time.

Campaigns range from 1 to 10 weeks depending on plan. Most artists see meaningful stream growth begin within the first 7–10 days as creator content starts circulating.

Our 100+ verified creators span music discovery, lifestyle, dance, and niche genre communities. Each is vetted for engagement quality, audience fit, and content authenticity before joining a campaign.

Results vary by genre, release quality, and plan size, but most clients see 5–25x growth in TikTok views and 3–10x growth in Spotify streams over the campaign window. Your dashboard shows live progress against benchmarks.

We can't guarantee virality — no one can. What we guarantee is consistent creator output, transparent reporting, and a strategy built around what's currently working on TikTok for your genre.
